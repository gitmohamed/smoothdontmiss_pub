ABOUT:


Born and raised in Compton, California, SMOOTH is a voice redefining R&B. His unique yet distinctive sound is quickly making him a standout act behind-the-scenes in Los Angeles. From session to session, producers and artists alike are catching wind that SMOOTH's voice and vibe will soon become an unparalleled force in the industry. His new single, "CHOOSE," produced by Tk Kayembe (prod for Kehlani, Masego, and more), Sam Yun, and B Major, is about relationships that are perceived as toxic on the outside looking in, yet are highly misunderstood.
